About Nethora

Our Story

Nethora is an online store founded in 2022 by Rhoneth Castillo, based in Calapan City, Philippines. The business has gained recognition for offering high-quality items and handmade accessories that reflect beauty through classic, timeless designs, all while keeping affordability in mind. Whether you're looking for the perfect accessory to complete your "Outfit of the Day" (OOTD) or a gift for a loved one, Nethora aims to be the go-to shop for every fashion enthusiast.

Their tagline, “Catered to Express Beauty,” encapsulates the brand's mission of helping customers feel confident and stylish with their accessory choices. Nethora has a booth at various locations, but they primarily operate online, making their products available for delivery via courier or in-store pickup. Payment options include PSBank, GCash, and cash, providing flexibility and convenience for customers.
